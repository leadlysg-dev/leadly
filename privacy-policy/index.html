  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, business name, and other details you provide when contacting us, booking calls, or using our services.
  • Usage Data: Information like IP address, browser type, device details, cookies, pages visited, and time spent on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

We apply strict security measures, including encryption, access control, and secure storage, to protect your data.

Leadly acts solely as a service provider and assumes no responsibility for any misuse or regulatory breach on the client's part.
