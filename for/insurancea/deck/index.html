Why the last one didn't work

The problem was never the leads.

It was everything that happened between the click and the call.

Reason 1

Nobody filtered anyone

A name and a number is not a lead. Your advisor finds out on the call — after they've spent the hour.

Reason 2

The lead went cold

Someone raises their hand at 9pm. Your advisor sees it Tuesday. By then they've spoken to three other people.

Reason 3

Nobody followed up twice

Most people aren't ready first time. If nothing chases them at 30 and 60 days, you paid for a lead and threw it away.

Reason 4

You couldn't see anything

The report was a screenshot. You had no idea which ad produced which lead, or if any of it worked.

The system

Five parts. One machine.

The system · 01 — Smart Qualifier

Not a name and a number.

The consumer-facing cousin of the questionnaire you just filled in. Your prospects answer it before they ever reach an advisor, so what lands in your team's hands is a person who has already told you what they need.

The system · 02 — Instant Ping

In your team's WhatsApp in under ten seconds.

The moment a lead finishes, the whole sales team gets pinged in one group — with every answer already attached. Whoever is free calls first, while the lead is still at their desk.

The system · 03 — Live Call Sheet

Every lead lands in one sheet. Your team just works it.

No exports. No "can you send me the latest list." Every lead writes itself in, with all the answers they gave, and updates as your team works it. Fuss-free.

The system · 04 — Winback Engine

You already paid for the ones who ghosted you.

Every lead cost you money, whether they picked up or not. We automate winback campaigns over the WhatsApp API, so they get worked again months later without anyone having to remember. Win back even five in a hundred and, over a year, that pays for itself many times over.

The system · 05 — Leadly Pulse

See exactly where your money goes.

Every build ships with UTM tagging, the Meta Pixel and server-side Conversions API wired in. Ad click, page view, questionnaire started, questionnaire submitted — every step is a tracked event, and all of it lands live in Pulse. Not a monthly PDF that arrives after the month it describes is over.

Where we stop We own the digital conversion. You own the sale.

We're accountable from the impression through to a qualified lead in your advisor's hand — the part we control, and the part we measure. What happens on the phone is your team's craft.
